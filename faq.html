Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of company is LoanMart?

LoanMart is an online financial solutions company dedicated to helping consumers and businesses across the globe to find highly rated solutions for their finance needs. Our services are provided without obligation. We are simply helping people who require funding for their situation they may not be able to find on their own. When you contact us, you are immediately connected to one of our senior loan specialist. They will ask you a few short questions to better understand your situation and help you to figure out which solution will best help you. We then help you achieve your financial goals.

How do i get started?

you can download loan application form (it will only take a few minutes to fill out) or you can reach us at 1-442-444-6988. Our knowledgeable and experienced financial specialists are available to help you with the application process

Is my information secure?

Your privacy is one of our top concerns. We safeguard your personal information in a variety of ways. You can review our privacy policy here

Do i need a collateral to secure a loan?

At LoanMart, you do not need a collateral to secure a loan regardless of the type of loan.We noticed how hard it is to secure a loan without sufficient or lack of collateral.That is why we have taken this initiative to bridge that gap. However after your loan application has been approved, you will be required to pay a small % of your loan amount as insurance to mitigate risk involved since no collateral will be collected from you.The loan insurance is refundable after your loan has been fully paid.

How long does it take to get approved?

Due to the high volumn of applications, there may be delays.Each applicant is emailed a decision within 2 to 3 business days after submitting an application.However, we may request for more information after submitting your application to better understand your situation and profer a solution to your needs.

How long will it take to receive my funds?

As soon as we confirm payment for your loan insurance, funds are approved and will be disbursed without further delay.

How do i receive payment?

For applicants within the united states, your approved loan will be sent via ACH, direct deposit, wire transfer or we can have a check mailed to your address.For applicants within other parts of the world, a wire transfer will be sent to your bank account.Note that for wire transfers outside the united states, it might take 2 to 5 business days to receive the funds in your bank account depending on your banking institution.

Do i need a business plan to get a business loan?

While other lenders might make it mandatory to have a business plan, we do not make it mandatory.However, having a formal business plan increases your chances of getting approved for a business loan.

Does your services include Mortgage loans

We do not provide mortgage loans at the moment.In the nearest future we hope to have it included in our services.All Customers will be informed as soon as we have Mortgage lending up and running.

We are your free online financial solutions resource for life. If you have questions or comments at any time, please visit the Contact Us page and you will be able to email us directly.
